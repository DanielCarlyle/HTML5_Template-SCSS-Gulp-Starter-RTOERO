Extended Health Care Plan

Our group health insurance pays for eligible expenses not normally covered by your Government Health Insurance Plan and includes retirement travel health coverage. More than 76,000 retired teachers and other education retirees and workers in Canada are covered by our plan.

Coverage and Benefits

Prescription drug coverage

The prescription drugs benefit covers drugs, sera, injectables, and compounds/mixtures with a Drug Identification Number (DIN), that require a prescription from a physician, dentist or practitioner and are dispensed by a licensed pharmacist.

  • $0 annual deductible
  • 85% reimbursement of ingredient costs
  • $3,400 annual maximum per person – we set the amount based on what members have needed
  • Reimbursement of Government Health Insurance Plan deductibles for prescription medications (e.g. ODB, RAMQ, and Fair Pharmacare)
  • Sexual dysfunction treatments and diabetic supplies for glucometers and insulin pumps are included
  • Option to arrange for a second 100-day drug supply if you’re taking an extended vacation

Paramedical practitioner coverage

Paramedical coverage includes non-surgical and surgical services. You don’t need a physician referral for services to be covered.

  • 80% reimbursement
  • $1,300 annual maximum per person for all practitioners combined
  • Covers 17 practitioners: acupuncturist, chiropodist, chiropractor
  • Dietician, herbalist, homeopath, naturopath, nutritionist, osteopath, physiotherapist, podiatrist, psychotherapist, registered clinical psychologist, registered massage therapist, shiatsu therapist, social worker, speech therapist
  • Includes acupuncture and foot care services when provided by a registered nurse
  • Reflexology services are eligible when performed by one of the covered paramedical practitioners within their scope of practice

Vision coverage

The plan includes prescription glasses and contact lenses, laser eye surgery and corneal incision. Plus, support is available for vision training or remedial exercises not covered by government plans.

  • 80% reimbursement
  • $400 per person for prescription eyewear and laser surgery in any two consecutive years
  • $150 per person for eye exams in any two consecutive years, and per calendar year for dependent children
  • An additional lifetime limit of $400 per person for new lenses required within six months of eye surgery
  • Lifetime maximum of $300 per eye for cataract surgery costs that aren’t covered elsewhere in the Extended Medical Plan

Aids and appliances coverage

The plan covers the costs of aids and appliances prescribed by a physician. Government plans can help offset costs. Our claims team will review your quote to help you determine what will be covered so you understand expenses before making a purchase.

  • 80% reimbursement
  • Includes trusses, splints, braces, casts, crutches, canes, walkers, wheelchairs and hospital beds
  • Covers artificial limbs or eyes and breast prosthesis, including three mastectomy bras per year
  • $640 per year for wigs
  • Medical supplies including $400 per person, per year for surgical stockings, $750 per person, per year for incontinence supplies and up to $200 per person every two consecutive calendar years for post-surgical comfort and convenience items
  • $500 per person for custom-made orthopedic shoes every two consecutive calendar years
  • $1,100 per person for purchase or repair of hearing aids every three successive calendar years

Diagnostic services coverage

The plan covers diagnostic lab tests, including screening tests. The coverage helps offset the costs of tests not covered by government plans.

  • 80% reimbursement
  • $250 annual maximum per person

Private duty nursing coverage

The plan covers medically necessary out-of-hospital nursing services. Additional related coverage is available through our Hospital and Convalescent Care Plan.

  • 80% reimbursement
  • $2000 per person every two consecutive calendar years

Transportation/ambulance coverage

The plan covers medically necessary ground and air transportation to a hospital.

  • 80% reimbursement
  • Includes one round trip per person per year for air transportation from your hospital to the nearest hospital able to provide treatment

Education program coverage

  • 80% reimbursement
  • Includes medically-related program(s) recommended by a physician to an annual maximum of $200 per person

Accidental dental coverage

The plan covers dental treatment required as a result of an accidental blow to natural or artificial teeth. For additional dental coverage, see our Dental Plan.

  • 80% reimbursement
  • Up to $1000 per person per calendar year

Retirement travel health coverage

The travel plan covers emergency medical expenses during out of province and out of country trips, and trip cancellation and interruption costs. You’re covered for up to 93 days and can add a Supplemental Travel Plan for your extended trips.

  • 100% reimbursement
  • Up to $10,000,000 for eligible medical emergency expenses per person, per trip
  • Unlimited number of trips
  • Up to $6000 for cancellation or interruption per person, per trip
  • Costs related to a pre-existing condition may be covered, subject to a 90-day medical stability clause
