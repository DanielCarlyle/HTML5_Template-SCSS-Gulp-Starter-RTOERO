RTO/ERO turns 50!

The Retired Teachers of Ontario is proudly celebrating 50 years in 2018. The organization started out as a grassroots initiative to look out for the pension needs and interests of retired teachers in Ontario.

It has now evolved into a 76,000 member strong organization that welcomes not only teachers but also all retirees from the broader education community. This means that if you are a human resources, communications professional, an early childhood educator or custodian you are eligible to join RTO/ERO.

RTO/ERO 50th Anniversary Merchandise Available

To celebrate our 50th, RTO/ERO is pleased to announce a variety of 50th Anniversary commemorative items, available for purchase through Souvenir Canada.

Members and non-members alike are encouraged to contact Souvenir Canada with their order or to download and complete an order form (PDF). Prices include all taxes. Shipping charges are extra: $5.50 for a single item and $9.50 for 2 or more items.
